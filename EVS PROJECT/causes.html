CAUSES OF WATER WASTAGE

Water wastage can be caused by a number of factors, including:

  • Overuse: Using too much water for pointless reasons
  • Leakage: Leaky pipes
  • Inefficient appliances: Conventional showerheads, toilets, and dishwashers
  • Water pollution: Sewage, wastewater, agricultural and industrial runoff, and pesticides
  • Climate change: Rising temperatures increase evaporation
  • Broken water lines: Broken or misaligned sprinkler heads, water lines, and valve boxes

Some other causes of water wastage include:

  • Watering lawns excessively
  • Using your toilet as a trash can
  • Taking baths and long showers
  • Laundry loads that are only half full
  • Running a dishwasher that's not completely full
  • Washing dishes with running water
  • Cleaning your sidewalks with a garden hose rather than a broom

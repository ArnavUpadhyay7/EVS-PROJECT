SOLUTIONS TO REDUCE WATER WASTAGE

Some ways to reduce the wastage of water are:

  • Check for leaks: Check pipes, taps, and toilets for leaks
  • Shorter showers: Take shorter showers and try to avoid baths
  • Reuse water: Reuse water used to wash vegetables to water garden plants
  • Don't run water: Don't let the water run while shaving or brushing teeth
  • Use less water when watering: Plant a low-water garden
